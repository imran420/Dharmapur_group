Message from the Chairman & CO.

AKM Zahirul Quyum is a business personality with more than 35 years of professional experience. In January 2016, he initiated DCIL with the resolution to run it as a family business that will run for generation dreaming to leave trail for his family. As the Chairman of the Dharmapur group, he is acting as CEO of DCIL to facilitate the company with his expertise and work with the staff and Board of Directors to oversee the strategic vision of the association as a whole. Mr. Khan began his career as a journalist ”Biporit Dhara” that is not published now a days that was later persuaded towards industry level manufacturing and import and export business.
