A little about us

Dharmapur Ceramics Industries Ltd is an Eco-Friendly Wall & Wall roof manufacturing company. We are introducing green brick manufacturing product under the Energy Kiln Project of Bangladesh Bank (BB) & refinanced bye Asian Development Bank (ADB),

Leader arranger Bangladesh Infrastructure Finance Fund Limited (BIFFL) including Bangladesh Commerce Bank Limited (BCBL). We have a collaboration agreement with Future Carbon. Our Certificate of Incorporation No: C-129683/2016. Our TIN No: 819611252143.

The advantage of tunnel kiln is that this technology is the most advanced tech. In green bricks production, it is helping to reduce the energy consumption resulting the less emission & smoke. The is most energy efficient & green technology in bricks production industry.

Necessity of this project

A little about the importance and impact of the project

Carbon emission is considered one of the most vulnerable problem globally. The technologies of the developed world are contributing most to this global phenomenon of high-carbon emissions. Innovation of energy efficient hi-tech is very important to increase the productivity in the industrial sector and also reduction of carbon emissions.

In this regard we have to innovate green technology for more efficient and less polluting industrial processes. A large number of brick kilns lack of standard level as required by the related law while many others do not have the clearances from Deportment of environment (DOE).Bricks are being manufactured in these traditional kilns are being manufactured in are burning firewood causing the imbalance of greens on the earth.

Furthermore, the smoke emitted from bricks kilns causes respiratory problem and asthma in children and elderly people in nearby areas. According to the UNDP survey, these brickfields cause air pollution, land degradation and deforestation.Bricks producers are major polluters, but today there is a cleaner alternative coming on-stream called green bricks.

Bangladesh has adopted smokeless bricks making technology introduced by UN development program. The innovative technology that is being used to replace the existing century-old system will make brick manufacturing more environment friendly in Bangladesh.
